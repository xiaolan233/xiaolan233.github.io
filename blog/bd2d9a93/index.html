题1


解: 由题意:
$ye^x=1+e^x$, $\Rightarrow$
$ye^x-e^x=1$, $\Rightarrow$
$(y+1)e^x=1$, $\Rightarrow$
$e^x=\dfrac{1}{y+1}$, $\Rightarrow$
$log_e\dfrac{1}{y+1}=x$
所以反函数$y=ln\dfrac{1}{y+1}$
