函数的和、差、积、商的求导法则

四则运算求导法则


如果函数$u=(x)$和$v=v(x)$都在点x可导,那么它们的和、差、积、商(除分母为0的点外)都在点$x$可导,且:

  • $(u \pm v)’=u’ \pm v’$
  • $(uv)’=uv’+uv’$
  • $(\dfrac{u}{v})’=\dfrac{u’v-uv’}{v^2}$,($v(x)\ne 0$)

题1


解:
$$
\begin{aligned}
y’=&2x\ln x+(\ln x)’x^2 \nonumber \\
=&2x\ln x+ \dfrac{1}{x} x^2 \nonumber \\
=&2x\ln x+ x \nonumber \\
\end{aligned}
$$

题2


解:
$$
\begin{aligned}
y’=&(\dfrac{\ln x}{x})’ \nonumber \\
=&\dfrac{(\ln x)’x- x’\ln x}{x^2} \nonumber \\
=&\dfrac{\dfrac{1}{x} x- \ln x}{x^2} \nonumber \\
=&\dfrac{1- \ln x}{x^2} \nonumber \\
\end{aligned}
$$

题3


解:特殊三角函数值
$y’=\cos x-(-\sin x )=\cos x+\sin x$
所以:
$\left. y’\right|_{x=\frac{\pi}{6}}
=\cos \dfrac{\pi}{6}+\sin \dfrac{\pi}{6}
=\dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+\dfrac{1}{2}
$

$\left. y’ \right|_{x=\frac{\pi}{4}}
=\cos \dfrac{\pi}{4}+\sin \dfrac{\pi}{4}
=\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}+\dfrac{\sqrt{2}}{2}
=\sqrt{2}
$
